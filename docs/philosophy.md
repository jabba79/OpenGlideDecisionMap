# Philosophy

## Pilot first

Every design decision must reduce pilot workload.

## Decision support

The map presents safe options, not complexity.

## Terrain first

Mountain flying follows terrain, not straight lines.

## Local knowledge

Experienced pilots possess knowledge that cannot be derived from terrain data alone.

OGDM captures and preserves this knowledge.

## Personal

Every pilot flies a different aircraft.  
Every pilot has different safety margins.  
Every pilot deserves a personal decision map.

## Paper first

The printed map is the primary product.  
Digital tools support its creation.

## Community

Pilots build the knowledge.  
Software builds the map.

## Open

Knowledge should be shared and continuously improved.
